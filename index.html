Travel in The Middle

A Destination Recommendation Service for Distant Couple

Get started

Do you have special one who lives far from you, and you find it difficult to find a new place for you two to go?

This web application will recommend you where to meet and travel given your current locations.

Get out of the comfort zone and find a place close to both of you

Rather than visiting some places and stay at one of yours house, the recommendation system will find some candidates of destination that they are all among a fair distance for both of you!
